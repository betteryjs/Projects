\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classObserver}{Observer}} Design Pattern

Intent\+: Lets you define a subscription mechanism to notify multiple objects about any events that happen to the object they\textquotesingle{}re observing.

Note that there\textquotesingle{}s a lot of different terms with similar meaning associated with this pattern. Just remember that the \mbox{\hyperlink{classSubject}{Subject}} is also called the Publisher and the \mbox{\hyperlink{classObserver}{Observer}} is often called the Subscriber and vice versa. Also the verbs \char`\"{}observe\char`\"{}, \char`\"{}listen\char`\"{} or \char`\"{}track\char`\"{} usually mean the same thing.
